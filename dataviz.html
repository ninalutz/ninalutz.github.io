Road to Paris

I worked in a team of 5 to design and make this website for our final project for our CMS class. Our website guides users through some short data visualizations and questions to teach them about climate change. Whether they are a skeptic or passionate supporter. The text of the website changes dynamically based off how they interact with the data visualizations and questions.

I was personally responsible for the overall outline of the website (design and concept) and I made the interactive visual components of the first two widgets and contributed to the last one where the user sees the effects of their own commute.
