DormBot

DorrmBot is an obstacle avoidance robot that my neighbor and I designed and are building together. This little guy simply roams our suite and avoids obstacles along the way. He’s enabled with a path finding algorithm to navigate the dorm and a network of different sensors to be aware of his surroundings on all axes and angles. In particular he uses IR and ultrasonic sensors to navigate the dorm.

Here’s a nice sketch of DormBot, who is expected to be all completed and troubleshot for our entry’s Thanksgiving celebration in November.
