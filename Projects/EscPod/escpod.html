EscPod

2017, MIT Media Lab - City Science Group
  • Design
  • Fabrication
  • Software
  • Electronics

The EscPod is a hackable, modular space built in the City Science research group. I designed, fabricated, and programmed an LED panel with the pod with a variety of interactive sequences and animations having to do with the archiecture and movements of the pod itself.

All work is by Nina Lutz, unless otherwise noted. Contact directly for use of photos or code.
