HI-BYE Stand

Fall 2016, Class Project
  • Design
  • Fabrication
  • CAD (Rhino)
  • Woodworking

This was my first time doing woodworking and even getting into furniture. I designed an end stand for my dorm room, one that would actually fit comfortably in the strange space between my bed and futon.



I went through a sketching and iteration process to get to this product.
