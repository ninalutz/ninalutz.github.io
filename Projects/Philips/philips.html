Philips Urban Lighting

Spring 2016, MIT Media Lab City Science Research
  • Design
  • Algorithms
  • UI
  • Data Analysis
  • GIS

I helped to design an interface and parse GIS data for an urban lighting project. I synthesized raw lux data from various sensors with GIS data in preparation for an agent based model. Apart from the data, I also helped to develop a model, some visualization ideas, and hand off a skeleton of ideas to summer researchers. I also helped to integrate and deploy this as a tactile interface with my supervisor Ira.

Ira Winder, the lead on this project, has posted it on Github.
