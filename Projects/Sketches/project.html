I have done almost daily sketches in p5.js on my art Twitter @ninasketches since 2019. These sketches have resulted in a variety of workshops was a 2021 winner of the Schnitzer Prize in the Visual Arts at MIT.

I also ran a digital workshop available here about some of the methods I utilize the most. Other workshops are posted on Twitter at an ad hoc basis.

2021 sketches are on this website 2022 is on this website.

I have additionally collaborated with the Paseo, a STEAM initiative and art foundation in New Mexico to offer free and accessible creative coding workshops to public high school students in the American Southwest.
