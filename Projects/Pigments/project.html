Pigments and Lumens

Reimagining lighting.

Pigments and Lumens

Present, MIT Media Lab Research
  • Design
  • Simulation
  • Lighting
  • Fabrication

This pedagogy of experiments seeks to accurately model and manufacture a new form of lighting fixtures and framework around architectural lighting. As well as creating artistic expression. This is a research project I am working on at the Media Lab, where you can read about it and the publication for it.

This project has been presented at Luxembourg Fashion Week 2019 and ACM BuildSys 2019.

All work is by Nina Lutz, unless otherwise noted. Contact directly for use of photos or code.
