Years Lost

Examining death deltas.

Years lost

Fall 2016, Class Project
  • Design
  • JavaScript
  • Graphics
  • Data Analysis
  • p5.js

This was a class assignment given with data from the Clinton foundation No Ceilings Project.

I was interested in the concept of a death delta, the difference and discrpenecies between the expected age and average age of death. I decided to look at this at the country level. So I parsed and analyzed the data to find this statistic and made this visualization for others to see it.
