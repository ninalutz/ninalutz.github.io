Part of a greater collaboration with GSK, my lab is working on two decision support systems. The first revolves around manufacturing and drug discovery, the second around smarter lab planning to increase synergy between scientists. Most of my work was on the previous project , but I acted as a research assistant on the manufacturing project too.

For manufacturing I helped to manage work and development sessions. I also helped deploy the table in London and added mouse interaction to the software. I assisted in configuring the computer vision and game mechanics for the table to run, built modular graph objects, and reorganized and polished the UI. A majority of this was done in a one week deployment in England with my supervisor.
