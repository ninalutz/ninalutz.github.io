Traffic Modeling

Fall 2016, Final Project
  • Software
  • Math Modeling
  • Algorithms

Traffic modeling project to explore transportation inequality by income. I worked in a team of 3 to formulate a math model and build a simulator in MATLAB. Utilized nodal analysis in both dynamic and steady state systems. I helped with the model and parsed Census and GIS data in Cambridge into an OD matrix for simulator. I visualized results in an interactive GUI written in Processing.
