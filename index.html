I'm a grad student at the MIT Media Lab. I'm in the Object Based Media Group. I think about spatial problems and cosmetics a lot. Not outer space, though.

I studied Computer Science and Design for undergrad at MIT, hanging out in Stata and studio. While earning my S.B, I spent 3 years at the Media Lab in City Science, where I did research with LEGOs and algorithms around architecture and city planning under Ira Winder. Between this I also spent a summer at Apple in Cupertino. I've also been president of an MIT dormitory and still give the occasional tour to prospective students via the Admissions office.

I also live on Instagram, Twitter, and my lab page . Come hang out or email me.

I am updating my work and CV and all those nice professional things. This website will be in constant flux for a bit, so please be patient and email me with questions.
