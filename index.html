I'm an incoming graduate student at the MIT Media Lab in the Object Based Media Group. This semester I'm completing my MIT undergrad in Computer Science and Design and hanging out at lab on the side.

I spent 3 years at the Media Lab in City Science, where I did research with LEGOs and algorithms around architecture and city planning under Ira Winder. Between this I also spent a summer at Apple in Cupertino. I've also been president of an MIT dormitory and still give the occasional tour to prospective students via the Admissions office.
