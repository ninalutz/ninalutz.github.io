Entertaining standards list!


This is a list of stuff all under $10 that I always like having around for entertaining purposes. I send this list to people in place of them bringing stuff to the event, because I always have too much food and other things people tend to bring around.


1. Disinfecting wipes

2. Dish soap

3. Paper towels

4. Napkins 

5. Hand soap 

6. Sharpies 

7. Cupcake liners

8. Nonstick cooking spray

9. Aluminum foil

10. Ziplock bags (all sizes are great) 

11. Tupperware or takeaway things

12. Disposable plates and cutlery
