4.031: Studio


I took this class at MIT my sophomore fall (Fall 2016). This class focuses on design and fabrication of three projects and integrating carpentry, electronics, 3D printing, programming, and other fabrication techniques to make designs a reality. All of this is done in a studio environment within the MIT Department of Architecture.

Overall I learned a lot from this class, from traditional woodwork to exploring generative design and 3D printing, and electronics. Most of my work from this class has been polished and can be found in the Projects section of my portfolio.

A complete record of my work from the class can be found here on the class's website.
