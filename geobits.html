GeoBits

This the project I'm leading within Changing Places at the MIT Media Lab under the direction of my supervisor Ira Winder .

GeoBits was my fall term project and I will be wrapping it up during MIT's IAP term (the month of January when we have no classes and can focus on projects and research). I will be writing a paper on the system during Spring semester. Please check back later for final documentation.

GeoBits seeks to be a user friendly platform where anyone can explore geospatial data and explore models anywhere in the world. These models include general flow models in event simulations, traffic, and pedestrian safety.
