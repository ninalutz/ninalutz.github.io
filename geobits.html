GeoBits

This the project I'm leading! Transforming, rendering, and standardizing geospatial data across different coordinate systems. Users can select anywhere in the world and export filtered geojsons of the information they need. And perform modeling simulations within the platform. Exploring math models for pedestrian accessibility in different areas based off demographic and path decisions. i.e; How walkable is Boston at night for a woman vs. a man? What about someone in a wheel chair? This project started in late August and will probably wrap up in December.
